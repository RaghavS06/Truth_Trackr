Connecting the World to Combat Misinformation

Inaccurate information spreads widely and at speed, making it more difficult for the public to identify verified facts and advice from trusted sources.

However, everyone can help to stop the spread. If you see content online that you believe to be false or misleading, you can report it to the hosting social media platform.
